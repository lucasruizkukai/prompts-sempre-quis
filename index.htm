Sempre Quis Importados

Guia de Prompts

Como criar posts no ChatGPT mantendo a identidade da marca

Como usar

1
Escolha o modelo certo pelas abas abaixo
2
Na caixa verde, escreva com suas palavras o que você quer postar
3
Clique em "Copiar prompt completo"
4
Cole no ChatGPT e anexe o logo + foto do produto (quando tiver)

Qual logo anexar

Logo extenso (Sempre Quis + IMPORTADOS): posts de feed, editoriais e tabelas.

Logo redondo (círculo roxo): stories e posts compactos.

MODELO 01

Post de Produto Simples

Quando usar

Divulgar 1 produto com foto, nome e preço

Anexar: logo extenso + foto do produto

✏️ Escreva aqui — só você mexe nisso

Descreva o produto que você quer postar:

Inclua: nome do produto, preço, tamanhos ou cores disponíveis, qualquer detalhe que queira destacar.

Parte fixa — não mexa abaixo
Prompt fixo da marca 
You are a luxury brand visual designer creating an Instagram product post for "Sempre Quis Importados", a Brazilian luxury imported goods brand (perfumes, cosmetics, bags, electronics, sneakers).

Use the product info described above and the attached product photo.
Use the attached brand logo exactly as provided — never recreate it.

OUTPUT: 1080x1350px, 300dpi, PNG, Instagram feed (4:5 portrait)

BACKGROUND: If product is dark-toned use #E8E5EB. If light-toned use #2A2A2A. Always clean and solid — never busy, textured or patterned.

PRODUCT: Hero of the image, minimum 55% of area. Center or slightly offset. One editorial angle only. No fake shadows or 3D effects.

TYPOGRAPHY: Elegant serif thin weight for product name and price. Color #E8E5EB on dark bg or #2A2A2A on light bg. No bold heavy fonts. Price format: R$ X.XXX,XX. Sizes/colors in spaced uppercase, color #918894.

LOGO: Bottom right corner, 15-18% of image width, discreet. Never distort proportions.

COLOR PALETTE (strict — no other colors): #2A2A2A #E8E5EB #551FBD #C6A75C #918894

RULES: Max 3 text elements. No emojis. No badges. No promotional text. Generous negative space. Feeling: editorial luxury magazine, not a marketplace listing.

REFERENCE: Dior Beauty, YSL Beauty, Guerlain Instagram posts.

MODELO 02

Tabela de Preços

Quando usar

Mostrar vários produtos e preços de uma vez

Anexar: logo extenso. Sem foto de produto.

✏️ Escreva aqui — só você mexe nisso

Descreva os produtos e preços que você quer na tabela:

Inclua: categoria, todos os itens com preços, algum aviso e data de validade.

Parte fixa — não mexa abaixo
Prompt fixo da marca 
You are a luxury brand visual designer creating a price list post for "Sempre Quis Importados", a Brazilian luxury imported goods brand.

Use the product and price info described above.
Use the attached brand logo exactly as provided — never recreate it.

OUTPUT: 1080x1920px, 300dpi, PNG, Instagram Stories/Feed vertical (9:16)

BACKGROUND: #1E1E1E main background. Never pure black. Subtle dark gradient to #2E063D allowed at edges only.

HEADER: Category name large, bold, all caps, color #E8E5EB. Thin gold line #C6A75C separating header from content.

PRICE CARDS: Each product gets its own card. Card bg: #2A2A2A. Card border: 1px #551FBD, thin and elegant. Product name: #E8E5EB bold. Specs: #918894 smaller. Price: #C6A75C (gold), large and prominent. Format: R$ X.XXX. Multiple price tiers: use divider lines #3D1280. Generous spacing between cards — never cramped.

DISCLAIMER AND DATE: Bottom area, color #918894. Small text.

LOGO: Footer area, centered, light version, gold separator line above.

COLOR PALETTE (strict): #1E1E1E #2A2A2A #2E063D #551FBD #C6A75C #E8E5EB #918894

RULES: No emojis. No rainbow colors. No neon. Never looks like a street market price list.

REFERENCE: Apple Store pricing meets luxury boutique aesthetic.

MODELO 03

Post Editorial

Quando usar

Lançamento, campanha ou vários produtos juntos

Anexar: logo extenso + fotos dos produtos

✏️ Escreva aqui — só você mexe nisso

Descreva o que você quer neste post editorial:

Inclua: tema ou campanha, marcas ou produtos que aparecem, clima que quer passar.

Parte fixa — não mexa abaixo
Prompt fixo da marca 
You are a luxury brand creative director creating an editorial campaign post for "Sempre Quis Importados", a Brazilian luxury imported goods brand (perfumes, cosmetics, bags, electronics).

Use the theme, products and mood described above and the attached product photos.
Use the attached brand logo exactly as provided — never recreate it.

OUTPUT: 1080x1350px, 300dpi, PNG, Instagram feed (4:5 portrait)

BACKGROUND: #E8E5EB for colorful products OR #2A2A2A for dark/dramatic mood. Subtle gradient allowed. Never busy, textured or competing with products.

PRODUCT COMPOSITION: Editorial arrangement — NOT a flat grid or catalog. Overlapping, varied heights, layered. Products styled as if by a professional photographer. 1 product: hero dominant. 2-4: editorial grouping. 5+: styled flat lay.

HEADLINE: Large, dominant, high-contrast serif (thick/thin strokes). Color: #2E063D on light bg or #E8E5EB on dark bg. Some words heavy, some light for editorial contrast. Wider letter spacing. Never rounded or generic sans-serif.

SUBHEADLINE: Clean light sans-serif, spaced, small, color #918894. Optional thin gold line or small star between headline and subheadline.

BRAND LISTING: Bottom, separated by bullets: Dior • YSL • KIKO. Spaced sans-serif, small, color #918894. Never list prices here.

LOGO: Bottom right, small and elegant — logo is a signature here.

COLOR PALETTE (strict): #E8E5EB #2A2A2A #2E063D #551FBD #C6A75C #918894

RULES: No price tags. No promotional badges. No emojis. No busy layouts. Feeling: Vogue Brasil or Harper's Bazaar page — not a store promotion.

REFERENCE: Sephora campaigns, Dior Beauty editorial, Net-a-Porter lookbook.

MODELO 04

Post Informativo

Quando usar

Explicar produto, benefícios ou dar dica ao seguidor

Anexar: logo extenso ou redondo. Sem foto necessária.

✏️ Escreva aqui — só você mexe nisso

Descreva o que você quer explicar neste post:

Inclua: assunto, tópicos ou informações que devem aparecer, tom que quer passar.

Parte fixa — não mexa abaixo
Prompt fixo da marca 
You are a luxury brand content designer creating an informative educational post for "Sempre Quis Importados", a Brazilian luxury imported goods brand.

Use the topic and content described above.
Use the attached brand logo exactly as provided — never recreate it.

OUTPUT: 1080x1350px for short content, 1080x1920px for longer content. Choose based on amount of information. 300dpi, PNG.

BACKGROUND: Educational mood: #E8E5EB (light, airy). Premium/authority mood: #2A2A2A (dark, prestigious). Never pure white or pure black. No textures or patterns.

HEADER: Main title large and bold. Color: #551FBD on light bg, #E8E5EB on dark bg. Thin gold separator line #C6A75C below title. Optional pill tag above title: bg #551FBD, white text, all caps spaced.

CONTENT SECTIONS: Each topic gets a thin line-style icon + bold title + short text. Icons: minimal, modern, color #551FBD — never cartoonish. Section title: semi-bold, #551FBD or #2E063D. Body text: light weight, max 2 lines per section. Never long paragraphs. Never cramped.

SPACING: Generous padding everywhere. Consistent spacing. If content is short: use larger text and more negative space.

LOGO: Bottom center or right, small, gold separator line above.

COLOR PALETTE (strict): #E8E5EB #2A2A2A #551FBD #2E063D #C6A75C #545157 #918894

RULES: No emojis. No prices. No promotional text. No bullet walls of text. Feeling: premium wellness brand guide — never looks like a pharmacy flyer.

REFERENCE: La Mer education posts, Elemis how-to, Sephora ingredient guides.
