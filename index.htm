Sempre Quis Importados

Guia de Prompts

Como criar posts no ChatGPT com a identidade da marca

Como usar

1
Escolha o formato: Feed ou Stories
2
Escolha o modelo certo pelas abas
3
Na caixa verde, escreva o que você quer postar
4
Clique em "Copiar prompt completo" e cole no ChatGPT
5
Anexe o logo + foto do produto (quando tiver)

Qual logo anexar

Logo extenso (Sempre Quis + IMPORTADOS): posts de feed, editoriais e tabelas.

Logo redondo (círculo roxo): stories e posts compactos.

MODELO 01

Post de Produto Simples

🖼 Feed 1080 x 1350px

Quando usar

Divulgar 1 produto com foto, nome e preço

Anexar: logo extenso + foto do produto

✏️ Escreva aqui — só você mexe nisso

Descreva o produto que você quer postar:

Inclua: nome do produto, preço, tamanhos ou cores disponíveis, qualquer detalhe que queira destacar.

Parte fixa — não mexa abaixo
Prompt fixo da marca
You are a luxury brand visual designer creating an Instagram FEED post for "Sempre Quis Importados", a Brazilian luxury imported goods brand.

Use the product info described above and the attached product photo.
Use the attached brand logo exactly as provided — never recreate it.

OUTPUT: 1080x1350px (4:5 portrait), 300dpi, PNG

BACKGROUND: If product is dark-toned use #E8E5EB. If light-toned use #2A2A2A. Clean and solid — never busy or textured.

PRODUCT: Hero of the image, minimum 55% of area. Center or slightly offset. One editorial angle. No fake shadows or 3D effects.

TYPOGRAPHY: Elegant serif thin weight for product name and price. Color #E8E5EB on dark bg or #2A2A2A on light bg. Price format: R$ X.XXX,XX. Sizes/colors in spaced uppercase, color #918894.

LOGO: Bottom right corner, 15-18% of image width, discreet. Never distort.

PALETTE (strict): #2A2A2A #E8E5EB #551FBD #C6A75C #918894

RULES: Max 3 text elements. No emojis. No badges. No promotional text. Generous negative space. Feeling: luxury magazine editorial, not a marketplace listing.

REFERENCE: Dior Beauty, YSL Beauty, Guerlain Instagram posts.

MODELO 02

Tabela de Preços

🖼 Feed 1080 x 1350px

Quando usar

Mostrar vários produtos e preços de uma vez

Anexar: logo extenso. Sem foto de produto.

✏️ Escreva aqui — só você mexe nisso

Descreva os produtos e preços que você quer na tabela:

Inclua: categoria, todos os itens com preços, aviso e data de validade.

Parte fixa — não mexa abaixo
Prompt fixo da marca
You are a luxury brand visual designer creating an Instagram FEED price list for "Sempre Quis Importados", a Brazilian luxury imported goods brand.

Use the product and price info described above.
Use the attached brand logo exactly as provided — never recreate it.

OUTPUT: 1080x1350px (4:5 portrait), 300dpi, PNG

BACKGROUND: #1E1E1E main. Subtle dark gradient to #2E063D allowed at edges only.

HEADER: Category name large, bold, all caps, color #E8E5EB. Thin gold line #C6A75C separating header from content.

PRICE CARDS: Each product gets its own card. Card bg: #2A2A2A. Border: 1px #551FBD. Product name: #E8E5EB bold. Specs: #918894 smaller. Price: #C6A75C large. Format: R$ X.XXX. Generous spacing between cards.

DISCLAIMER: Bottom area, color #918894, small text.

LOGO: Footer, centered, light version, gold separator line above.

PALETTE (strict): #1E1E1E #2A2A2A #2E063D #551FBD #C6A75C #E8E5EB #918894

RULES: No emojis. No neon colors. Never looks like a street market price list.

REFERENCE: Apple Store pricing meets luxury boutique aesthetic.

MODELO 03

Post Editorial

🖼 Feed 1080 x 1350px

Quando usar

Lançamento, campanha ou vários produtos juntos

Anexar: logo extenso + fotos dos produtos

✏️ Escreva aqui — só você mexe nisso

Descreva o que você quer neste post editorial:

Inclua: tema ou campanha, marcas ou produtos, clima que quer passar.

Parte fixa — não mexa abaixo
Prompt fixo da marca
You are a luxury brand creative director creating an editorial campaign FEED post for "Sempre Quis Importados", a Brazilian luxury imported goods brand.

Use the theme, products and mood described above and the attached product photos.
Use the attached brand logo exactly as provided — never recreate it.

OUTPUT: 1080x1350px (4:5 portrait), 300dpi, PNG

BACKGROUND: #E8E5EB for colorful products OR #2A2A2A for dark dramatic mood. Never busy or textured.

PRODUCT COMPOSITION: Editorial arrangement — NOT a flat grid. Overlapping, varied heights, layered. Styled as if by a professional photographer.

HEADLINE: Large, dominant, high-contrast serif. Color: #2E063D on light or #E8E5EB on dark. Editorial contrast between heavy and light words. Never rounded or generic fonts.

SUBHEADLINE: Clean light sans-serif, small, color #918894.

BRAND LISTING: Bottom, separated by bullets: Brand • Brand • Brand. Color #918894. Never list prices here.

LOGO: Bottom right, small, signature-style.

PALETTE (strict): #E8E5EB #2A2A2A #2E063D #551FBD #C6A75C #918894

RULES: No price tags. No promotional badges. No emojis. Feeling: Vogue Brasil editorial page.

REFERENCE: Sephora campaigns, Dior Beauty editorial, Net-a-Porter lookbook.

MODELO 04

Post Informativo

🖼 Feed 1080 x 1350px

Quando usar

Explicar produto, benefícios ou dar dica ao seguidor

Anexar: logo extenso ou redondo. Sem foto necessária.

✏️ Escreva aqui — só você mexe nisso

Descreva o que você quer explicar neste post:

Inclua: assunto, tópicos que devem aparecer, tom que quer passar.

Parte fixa — não mexa abaixo
Prompt fixo da marca
You are a luxury brand content designer creating an informative FEED post for "Sempre Quis Importados", a Brazilian luxury imported goods brand.

Use the topic and content described above.
Use the attached brand logo exactly as provided — never recreate it.

OUTPUT: 1080x1350px (4:5 portrait), 300dpi, PNG

BACKGROUND: Educational mood: #E8E5EB (light, airy). Premium mood: #2A2A2A (dark, prestigious). Never pure white or black. No textures.

HEADER: Main title large and bold. Color: #551FBD on light, #E8E5EB on dark. Thin gold separator line #C6A75C below title.

CONTENT SECTIONS: Each topic: thin line-style icon + bold title + max 2 lines of text. Icons: minimal, modern, color #551FBD. Never cartoonish. Never long paragraphs.

SPACING: Generous padding. Consistent spacing between sections.

LOGO: Bottom center or right, small, gold separator line above.

PALETTE (strict): #E8E5EB #2A2A2A #551FBD #2E063D #C6A75C #545157 #918894

RULES: No emojis. No prices. No promotional text. Feeling: premium wellness brand guide.

REFERENCE: La Mer education posts, Sephora ingredient guides.
You are a luxury brand content designer creating an informative STORY for "Sempre Quis Importados", a Brazilian luxury imported goods brand.
